HR Attrition Analysis Dashboard | Power BI

Objective:
To analyze employee attrition patterns and identify the key drivers behind workforce exits, enabling HR teams to take targeted retention actions.
Tools Used
•	Power BI 
•	MS Excel 
Key Insights
•	R&D department recorded the highest attrition among all departments. 
•	Employees aged 26–35 showed the highest attrition levels. 
•	Attrition peaked during the first two years of employment. 
•	Laboratory Technicians showed the highest early attrition risk. 
•	Overtime significantly increased attrition across satisfaction levels. 
Business Recommendation
Introduce focused retention initiatives for R&D employees in their first two years, optimize overtime policies, and improve onboarding support for high-risk technical roles.
